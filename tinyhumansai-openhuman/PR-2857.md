## AI Summary
- **What it does**: Fixes a UX bug where long-running sub-agent operations (delegated Research/Tools Agent/Memory Tree turns) incorrectly triggered the 120s safety timeout toast even while tools were visibly firing in the timeline. The rearm effect now watches `toolTimelineByThread` and `taskBoardByThread` in addition to `inferenceStatusByThread` and `streamingAssistantByThread`. Introduces `prevInferenceStatusRef` to distinguish "status not yet arrived post-send" from "status was cleared by chat completion" — preventing the Send handler's `setToolTimelineForThread({ entries: [] })` call from immediately tearing down the just-armed timer.
- **Breaking risk**: Zero — internal React state management change only. No public APIs, shared types, DB schemas, or exported functions changed.
- **Security risk**: Zero — timer logic in a UI component.
- **Bottom line**: Safe to merge once the two ref-reset issues and CI are resolved.
