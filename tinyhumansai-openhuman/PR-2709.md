## AI Summary
- **What it does**: Removes the process-global `OPENHUMAN_CORE_TOKEN` env var as the transport for the per-launch RPC bearer in the Tauri embedded-core path. Instead passes the bearer directly in-memory through `run_server_embedded_with_ready(rpc_token: Some(_))` → `init_rpc_token_with_value`. Closes a known local-privilege-escalation-adjacent information exposure tracked in docs/SECURITY_AUDIT.md. Standalone CLI/Docker/cloud paths are explicitly unchanged.
- **Breaking risk**: Low — internal transport change only; all external-facing APIs and env-as-config surfaces are preserved.
- **Security risk**: Low — this PR *reduces* attack surface by closing the same-UID readback channel. No new security issues introduced.
- **Bottom line**: Safe to merge once CI is green.
